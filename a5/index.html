New York Strip Steak

A cooked New York steak served in the pan with caramelized onions and fresh garnishes. The cut is from the short loin section of the cow.

Porterhouse Steak

A cooked and sliced porterhouse steak with fresh parsley garnishing. The cut is from the rear end of the short loin. It offers two textures within the same steak.

Ribeye Steak

A cooked ribeye steak topped with butter. The cut is from the rib section, and is known for being both incredibly tender and flavorful.

T-Bone Steak

A cooked medium-rare T-bone steak, featuring both the strip and tenderloin sections. The cut, similar to the porterhouse is from the short loin. However, it's closer to the front than the porterhouse.

Beef Tenderloin

A cooked and sliced tenderloin steak. The cut is from the loin section, and is known for being the most tender cut of beef, which provides a soft and buttery texture.
